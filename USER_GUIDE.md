# 🎸 Ukulele App - User Guide (v1.0)

Welcome to your digital ukulele songbook! This one-pager explains how to get the most out of the app in its current state.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started
1. **Sign In:** Click **"Get Started with Google"** on the landing page to create your private cloud-synced account.
2. **Dashboard:** Once logged in, you'll see the **Song Builder** on the left and **Your Library** on the right.

---

## 📝 Building a Song
The Song Builder is where you create or update your tracks.

*   **Song Title:** The name of your song.
*   **Lyrics & "Smart Chords":** Type your lyrics and put chords in brackets immediately before the syllable where they change.
    *   *Example:* `[C]Row, row, row your [G]boat...`
*   **Chords Used (Important!):** List the chords used in the song (e.g., `C, G, Am, F`). This field is used for the **Search** functionality.
*   **Style (Strumming & BPM):** Enter your preferred strumming pattern (e.g., `D-D-U-U`) and the tempo (BPM).
*   **YouTube URL:** Paste a link to a tutorial or a cover to have it handy while you practice.

---

## 🎤 Performance Mode
When you load a song from your library, it opens in a clean, professional view.

*   **View Toggle:** Use the switch at the top to flip between **Edit Mode** (writing) and **Performance Mode** (playing).
*   **Visual Chords:** In Performance Mode, chords appear in blue directly above the lyrics.
*   **Metadata:** Your BPM, Strumming, and YouTube link are pinned to the top of the performance sheet.
*   **Dynamic Diagrams:** The app automatically generates visual fretboard diagrams for every chord it detects in your brackets.

---

## 🔍 Managing Your Library
Your library stores all your songs in the cloud.

*   **Search by Chord:** Type a chord (e.g., `G`) into the search box. The library will instantly filter to show only songs that contain that chord in their "Chords Used" list.
*   **Clear Filter:** Click the "Clear Filter" link to return to your full song list.
*   **Edit / Perform:** Click any song card to load it back into the builder.
*   **Delete:** Remove songs permanently from your cloud library.

---

## 💡 Quick Pro-Tips
*   **Case Sensitivity:** Search is case-insensitive, so `am` will find `Am`.
*   **Partial Search:** Searching for `7` will find all your 7th chords (G7, C7, etc.).
*   **External Content:** Clicking "Watch on YouTube" opens the video in a new tab so you don't lose your place in the app.

---

*Happy playing!* 🎶
